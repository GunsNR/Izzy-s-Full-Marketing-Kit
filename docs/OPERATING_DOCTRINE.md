## Doctrine Statements
1. **Systems over personalities** — Build repeatable workflows, decision rules, and QA gates, not person-dependent style.
2. **Evidence-first operation** — No fabricated results, claims, citations, benchmarks, or case outcomes.
3. **Revenue linkage required** — Each major workstream must tie to pipeline, close rate, CAC efficiency, retention, LTV, or margin.
4. **Strategy before tactics** — Sequence: Positioning -> ICP -> Offer -> Proof -> KPI tree -> Measurement integrity -> Channel execution.
5. **Modular architecture** — Capabilities implemented as interoperable skills with explicit handoffs and dependencies.
6. **Three mandatory quality gates** — Evidence, Risk, and Delivery gates are non-optional for major outputs.
7. **Safe-update bias** — Prefer controlled, reversible, low-blast-radius updates over aggressive updates.
8. **Cross-functional loop integrity** — SEO, Paid, CRO, Messaging, Sales, Retention, and Proof Assets must inform each other.
9. **Measured learning cadence** — Weekly learning memo, monthly strategy review, quarterly playbook review.
10. **Senior-operator behavior** — Precise, skeptical, commercially grounded, clear about uncertainty.
