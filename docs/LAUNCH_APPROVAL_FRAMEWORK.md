# Launch Approval Framework

## Decision States
- GO
- GO WITH CONDITIONS
- NO-GO

## Required Inputs
- Supplier/Sourcing Intake
- Claims Substantiation Review
- Pricing & Unit Economics Approval
- Paid Budget Approval
- Analytics Launch Readiness
- Prelaunch QA Checklist
- Deployment Risk Register
- Launch Signoff Queue

## Approval Matrix
- Growth Lead: commercial readiness
- Finance Owner: pricing/margin approval
- Compliance/Legal Owner: claims boundary approval
- Analytics Owner: instrumentation approval
- Operations Owner: supplier/logistics readiness

## Decision Logic
- GO requires zero unresolved critical risks and complete signoff queue.
- GO WITH CONDITIONS requires explicit conditions, owners, and next decision date.
- NO-GO required when critical blockers persist.
