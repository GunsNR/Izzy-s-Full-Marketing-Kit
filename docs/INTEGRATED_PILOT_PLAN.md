# Integrated Pilot Plan

## Pilot Definition
- **Offer:** SEO + Paid + CRO Growth Sprint (8 weeks)
- **ICP:** B2B SaaS companies (ARR $1M-$20M) with plateauing demo pipeline
- **Autonomy mode:** Hybrid (execution with human checkpoints on high-risk changes)

## Pilot Workstreams
1. Positioning refresh (positioning-and-offer-strategy)
2. Customer research sprint (customer-research-and-voc)
3. SEO quick wins (seo-technical-audit + content-strategy-engine)
4. One paid campaign (paid-search-operator)
5. One landing page (landing-page-builder)
6. Two experiments (experimentation-program-manager)
7. One nurture sequence (lifecycle-nurture-builder)
8. Measurement + narrative reporting (measurement-reporting-operator)

## Measurement Plan
Primary KPIs: qualified pipeline, LP CVR, CAC efficiency, SQL rate, close rate trend.

## Reporting Standard
Every checkpoint uses:
- What we learned
- What we will do next
- What we will stop

## Gate Controls
- Risk gate approval required for major budget, tracking, pricing, or site-wide SEO changes.
