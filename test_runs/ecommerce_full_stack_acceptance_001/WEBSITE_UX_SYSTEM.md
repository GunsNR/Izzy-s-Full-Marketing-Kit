# WEBSITE_UX_SYSTEM

## Objective
Design a conversion-oriented storefront architecture that supports first purchase + subscription retention.

## Site Map
- Home
- Shop (Variety Pack, Bundles, Subscribe)
- How It Works
- Ingredients
- Reviews
- FAQ
- About
- Journal

## Homepage Strategy
1. Hero: one-sentence value prop + pack shot + protocol CTA.
2. Problem-solution block (energy dips, convenience, consistency).
3. Proof block (ingredient transparency, UGC snippets, review highlights).
4. Bundle/subscription block.
5. FAQ and risk-reversal close.

## PDP Structure
- Above fold: problem-aware headline, social proof count, flavor selector, subscription default.
- Middle: ingredient rationale, use protocol, taste expectations, comparisons.
- Bottom: objections, FAQs, shipping/returns trust markers.

## CRO Hypotheses
- **Probable:** Subscription pre-selection with clear skip/cancel language increases LTV without increasing refund risk.
- **Provisional:** "Desk-to-Distance" bundle may increase AOV by >15%.

## Cart / Checkout Friction Controls
- Keep one-page checkout.
- Show delivery timing and cancellation policy above payment step.
- Trigger abandoned-cart sequence at 30 min and 20 hours.
