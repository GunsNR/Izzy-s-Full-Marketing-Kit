# STRATEGY_FOUNDATION

## Objective
Define ICP, positioning, offer, proof path, objections, and KPI tree for a launchable hydration-stick DTC company.

## ICP and Segments
1. Commuter Athletes (25-40): train before/after office, convenience-first.
2. Desk-to-Gym Operators: long workdays, energy/hydration dips, want low-sugar function.
3. Recreational Endurance Community: weekend races, hydration rituals, social proof driven.

## Pains / Jobs / Desires
- **Probable:** Need energy and hydration without sugary sports drinks.
- **Probable:** Want portable format and predictable taste.
- **Provisional:** Most compelling emotional driver by segment requires survey/interview validation.

## Positioning
"Performance hydration for people who train around real life schedules."

## Offer Structure
- Core: 30-stick variety pack.
- AOV lever: 2-pack bundle with shaker bottle.
- LTV lever: subscribe-and-save at 15% with flavor rotation.

## Pricing Logic
- **Provisional:** Target DTC gross margin >= 65% at list price, >=55% after discounts.
- **Risk gate:** Final pricing requires human approval after supplier + shipping quote validation.

## Proof Strategy
- Proof ladder: formulation transparency -> founder protocol demo -> UGC field-use clips -> verified reviews.
- No medical/performance guarantees without substantiation.

## Objection Map
- "Will it taste artificial?" -> flavor development and third-party taste panel snippet.
- "Is this just expensive salt?" -> ingredient quality + dose rationale visual.
- "Will I actually feel a difference?" -> protocol expectations + honest response windows.

## KPI Tree
- Revenue -> Sessions x CVR x AOV.
- LTV -> Repeat rate x subscription retention.
- Efficiency -> MER, CAC payback, contribution margin.

## Next Action
Run brand system and website architecture to convert this strategy into customer-facing assets.

## Assumptions
- **Probable:** Convenience + taste adherence are primary purchase drivers for this audience.
- **Provisional:** Segment size mix and willingness-to-pay bands require live testing.

## Source Clarity
- **Verified:** KPI tree and objection map are internally consistent with repository templates.
- **Provisional:** No external market datasets were used in this environment.

## Dependencies
- Packaging and flavor validation samples.
- CRM/analytics implementation resources.

## Rollback
If early cohorts underperform, simplify to single flagship flavor and revise offer stack.
